Icosahedra is Brian Kehrer's independent game development studio, based in Brooklyn, NYC.

Brian is a game designer and developer. He was a co-founder of Muse Games, and Game Director for the multiplayer airship combat game, Guns of Icarus Online, from concept through launch.

In 2012, Brian joined Psyop, where he worked as technical game director, and created Nightmare: Malaria, a game for change which has received over 1.2 million downloads.

Brian is currently working on independent projects and contracting.
